About Me

Hello, my name is Vickie Yim. I am a user experience and visual designer residing in Vancouver, Canada. And I am a soon-to-be graduate with a Bachelors of Science degree from Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts & Technology. In addition to experinece and inferface design, I also enjoy working on graphic design, web development (HTML and CSS), and videography.

The most important thing in design to me is to create long-term solutions, by learning from our past, and to do things better the next time. A topic that I am passionate about is environmental preservation and awareness.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing volleyball, hiking and travelling. My last big adventure out of the country was backpacking through Southeast Asia. If you want to check out some of my travel photos, head over to my Behance page!
