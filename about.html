about me

My name is Vickie, and I am product designer from Vancouver, BC. I am currently in school, studing in the Interactive Arts & Technology program at Simon Fraser University.

I strive to use interface and experience design to create a narrative with technology; something that is so simple, yet delicate, that the tiniest details make all the different. And to do so, I believe we need to constantly be learning, creating, and redefining what we think we already know.

I am always up for trying something new, whether it is within my field or not. In my spare time, I like to do a lot of exploring and going on adventures. In Beautiful British Columbia, you can find me going out on a lot of hikes (rain or shine). My last big adventure out of the country was backpacking through Europe (that's me in The Roman Colsseum in the photo above).
