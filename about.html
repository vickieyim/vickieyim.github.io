About Me

Hello! My name is Vickie, and I am a user experience and interface designer from Vancouver, BC (Canada). I am currently studying in the Interactive Arts & Technology program at Simon Fraser University.

I strive to create design experiences that fully encompass a deep understanding of a situation, to create long-term solutions rather than a temporary fix. To do so, I believe we need to stay open minded, constantly learning, creating, and redefining what we think we know.

In addition to (UI/UX) design, I also enjoy working on graphic design, web development (HTML/CSS), and a bit of videography.

In my spare time, I like going exploring, such as going on a hike or travelling around the world, and playing volleyball. My last big adventure out of the country was backpacking through Southeast Asia.
